# Physical-Kit Checklist

| Item | Suggested product / spec | Qty | Notes |
|------|--------------------------|-----|-------|
| **All‑in‑one printer with email‑printing** | HP DeskJet 4255e (Wi‑Fi, AirPrint, Instant Ink) – <https://a.co/d/0929M8wk> | 1 | Keep a spare cartridge & an extra tray of paper in the box. |
| **Paper** | 8.5×11 in, 20‑lb, white | 1 ream (500 sheets) | Store flat to avoid jams. |
| **Ink** | HP 67 Black + HP 67 Color (or Instant Ink subscription) | 2 each (spare) | Replace before each event. |
| **Wi‑Fi / Hotspot** | Mobile hotspot (e.g., Verizon Jetpack, AT&T Nighthawk) | 1 | 4G/5G data plan with at least 5 GB/mo. |
| **QR‑code holder** | Acrylic sign holder (2 × 8 in) – <https://a.co/d/0aMJjqup> | 1 | Insert the QR‑code printout (see Section 3). |
| **Box / tote** | Sturdy rolling toolbox or heavy‑duty tote | 1 | Label “Non‑Parent Caregiver Kit”. |
| **Pens** | Box of low‑cost ballpoint pens (blue/black) | 1 (≈30 pcs) | Keep one pen per participant. |
| **Power strips** | 2‑outlet surge‑protected strips with long cords | 2 | Plug printer, hotspot, laptop, etc. |
| **Table** | 4‑ft folding table (or any flat surface) | 1 | Optional if venue provides a table. |
| **Chairs** | Lightweight folding chairs | 3 | For volunteers / participants. |
| **Notary public (Texas)** | Certified Texas notary – on‑site or on‑call via video notarization | 1 per event | Must have a current commission & seal. |
| **Optional Texas lawyer** | Family‑law attorney (pro‑bono) for Q&A | 0‑1 per event | Not required for notarization. |
| **Interpreter (multi‑language)** | Bilingual volunteer or on‑demand phone app (e.g., Google Translate) | As needed | Spanish is mandatory; add others per community. |
| **Laptop / tablet** | For printing & displaying QR‑code | 1 | Can be a phone with email‑print capability. |
| **Sign‑in sheet** | Simple paper log (name, phone, email, consent) | 1 | For follow‑up & data tracking (HIPAA‑safe). |
| **Sanitizing wipes** | Alcohol‑based wipes | 1 pack | Clean printer & surfaces between participants. |
