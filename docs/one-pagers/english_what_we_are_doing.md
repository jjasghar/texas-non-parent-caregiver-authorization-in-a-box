
# FREE HELP – TX FM‑34‑109 (Non‑Parent Caregiver Authorization)

## Why you might need this form
- To name a trusted adult who can make medical, school, and legal decisions for your child if you become unable to do so.
- It’s required by schools, doctors, and many agencies.
- No cost – we’ll help you fill it out, print it, and have it notarized today.

## How it works
1. Scan the QR code below or use the link on your phone to open the online form.
1. Fill out the information with our volunteer’s assistance (Spanish interpreter available).
1. Print the completed form, sign, and have a Texas notary public notarize it on the spot.

## What you get
- A completed, notarized FM‑34‑109 that you keep.
- Copies for your doctor, school, and any other agency that asks.

We’re here to protect your children. Ask a volunteer any question!
