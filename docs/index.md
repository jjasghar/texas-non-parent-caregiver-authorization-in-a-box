# Non‑Parent Caregiver Authorization – In‑a‑Box

A portable, low‑cost outreach kit that lets volunteers help Texas families fill out, print, and notarize FM‑34‑109 (Authorization Agreement for Voluntary Non‑Parent Caregiver) on the spot.

# ADD QR CODE TO THE DOCASSMEBLE INSTANCE

## Project Overview

To make the FM-34-109 easy to complete for parents to protect their children.

## Steps

1. Order the hardware and print the QR‑code & one‑pagers.
1. Reach out to venues with the [form letter](one-pagers/request_to_host_form_letter.md).
1. Schedule your event, recruit volunteers.
1. On the day of the event, be friendly, help the best you can, and know you are helping make more children safe.
